01 The mission

Putting the game back where it belongs.

In the 1970s and 80s, table tennis was commonly seen playing in every street in Lagos as a household game. The reverse is the case today.

Value Sports One develops sport from the grassroots — specialising in table tennis development and equipment. Through our Table Tennis Education Programme we provide free tables, rackets, balls and an assigned coach to interested schools gearing up for the School League. (Terms and conditions apply.)

Mr. Adelaja George Ijaodola Director, Value Sports One

05 Why table tennis

A small game with big benefits.

More than fun — one of the best sports for body, mind and social wellbeing, for all ages and all levels.

Physical

  • Improves cardiovascular fitness
  • Burns 300–500 calories per hour
  • Strengthens core, legs and arms
  • Improves balance, agility and coordination
  • Develops fast-twitch muscles and endurance
  • Low impact, low injury risk

Mental & cognitive

  • Enhances concentration and focus
  • Improves memory and mental sharpness
  • Boosts decision-making and strategic thinking
  • Increases brain blood flow (up to 50%)
  • May help delay cognitive decline and dementia

Emotional

  • Reduces stress and anxiety
  • Boosts mood and self-confidence
  • Releases endorphins — the feel-good hormones
  • Builds resilience and emotional well-being

Social

  • Encourages friendship and connection
  • Brings people of all ages together
  • Builds teamwork, respect and communication
  • Creates a sense of community and belonging

Great for every age

Children

Improves motor skills, coordination, focus, discipline and confidence — while making exercise fun.

Adults

Supports fitness, reduces stress, improves concentration and increases everyday energy.

Seniors

Maintains mobility, balance, memory and independence for a healthier, fuller life.
